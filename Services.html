Our Services

Alande Vikas Karyakari Credit Society Ltd. endows its customers with a wide array of short term as well as long term deposit schemes with an attractive interest rate of 11%. To cater the urban and rural areas with micro finance, the institution instigated the Daily Deposit Scheme which is executed through 'Bank at your Doorstep' concept with a strong team of 825 marketing representatives all over Maharashtra who are efficiently catering to this field work with a collection of Rs. 2 Crores on a daily basis.
